Players ask what the [Zadeyo Valorant build](https://zadeyo.com/valorant-xray) includes. This article walks through **each toggle** in the loader and how it looks in Valorant—matching the video above.

## 1. Wall ESP (enemy boxes)

**What it does:** draws a box around enemy players, visible through walls and abilities that block sight.

**How to use it well:**

- Keep box thickness **low** in ranked—thick neon boxes block your own crosshair.
- Use contrasting colors per team if the loader supports per-side colors.

**What it does not do:** show friendly teammates unless you enable team ESP (leave off unless you coach).

## 2. Valorant wallhack

**What it does:** the valo wall hack overlay shows enemy positions through walls, smokes, and utility—core to most **valorant hacks**.

**Our recommendation:** pair wallhack with thin boxes first. Add skeleton ESP once you are comfortable.

## 3. Health bars

**What it does:** shows remaining HP on tagged enemies.

**Why it matters in Valorant:**

- Finish kills on low-HP agents instead of reloading into a full team.
- Decide whether to commit utility on a 40 HP target behind cover.

**Limitation:** HP can lag a tick behind burst damage. Do not treat it like a tournament API feed.

## 4. Distance (meters)

**What it does:** prints distance from you to each enemy.

**Practical uses:**

- Vandal falloff — stop shooting when distance says you are out of effective range.
- Rotate timing — know if the flanker is 5m vs 15m before you turn.

## 5. Snaplines (optional)

**What it does:** draws a line from screen center (or crosshair) to the enemy.

**Our recommendation:** leave **off** until you are used to boxes. Snaplines plus boxes can clutter the screen in 5v5 fights.

## 6. Custom colors & thickness

Ranked profile suggestion:

- Box color: soft magenta or cyan (not pure white)
- Thickness: 1–2 px
- Background fill: off

Unrated / clips:

- Slightly brighter colors for visibility on YouTube—still toggle off before main menu if you record.

## 7. Saved profiles

Store at least:

| Profile | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| `ranked` | Minimal clutter |
| `unrated` | Slightly louder colors |
| `warmup` | Max info for shooting range testing |

Switch profiles in the loader before you queue—do not edit mid-match unless you are dead in spawn.

## 8. Menu toggle key

Bind a key you can hit without looking (function keys work). Use it when:

- Opening the Riot Client overlay
- Recording a clean clip
- Talking to friends in Discord screen share

This is not "stream proof"—OBS can still capture ESP if you forget to toggle off.

## Aimbot, triggerbot, and skeleton ESP

**Skeleton ESP** draws bones on the model—handy when agents animate fast.

**Valorant aimbot** is a smooth assist with FOV and strength sliders. Tune it low in ranked if you want subtle help.

**Valorant triggerbot** fires when your crosshair crosses an enemy—adjust delay so it does not look robotic.
