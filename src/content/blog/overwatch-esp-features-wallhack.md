Players ask what the [Zadeyo Novaxware build](https://zadeyo.com/products/overwatch-2-novaxware) includes. This article walks through **each toggle** in the loader and how it looks in Overwatch 2—matching the video above.

## 1. Wall ESP (enemy boxes)

**What it does:** draws a box around enemy players, visible through walls and abilities that block sight.

**How to use it well:**

- Keep box thickness **low** in ranked—thick neon boxes block your own crosshair.
- Use contrasting colors per role if the loader supports per-team colors (e.g. red DPS, purple support).

**What it does not do:** show friendly teammates unless you enable team ESP (leave off unless you coach).

## 2. Health bars

**What it does:** shows remaining HP on tagged enemies.

**Why it matters in OW2:**

- Finish kills on squishies instead of reloading into a full HP tank.
- Supports can decide whether to commit cooldowns on a 40 HP target behind cover.

**Limitation:** HP is what the overlay reads from the game state—it can lag a tick behind burst damage. Do not treat it like a tournament API feed.

## 3. Distance (meters)

**What it does:** prints distance from you to each enemy.

**Practical uses:**

- Hitscan falloff (Soldier 76, Cassidy) — stop shooting when distance says you are out of effective range.
- Dive timing — know if the Winston on your flank is 5m vs 15m before you turn.

## 4. Snaplines (optional)

**What it does:** draws a line from screen center (or crosshair) to the enemy.

**Our recommendation:** leave **off** until you are used to boxes. Snaplines plus boxes can clutter the screen in six-stack fights.

## 5. Custom colors & thickness

Ranked profile suggestion:

- Box color: soft magenta or cyan (not pure white)
- Thickness: 1–2 px
- Background fill: off

Quick play / clips:

- Slightly brighter colors for visibility on YouTube—still toggle off before main menu if you record.

## 6. Saved profiles

Store at least:

| Profile | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| `ranked` | Minimal clutter |
| `qp` | Slightly louder colors |
| `warmup` | Max info for practice range testing |

Switch profiles in the loader before you queue—do not edit mid-match unless you are dead in spawn.

## 7. Menu toggle key

Bind a key you can hit without looking (function keys work). Use it when:

- Opening Battle.net overlay
- Recording a clean clip
- Talking to friends in Discord screen share

This is not “stream proof”—OBS can still capture ESP if you forget to toggle off. Be honest with your audience.

## Skeleton, head ESP, and soft aim

**Skeleton ESP** draws bones on the model—handy when heroes animate fast.

**Head ESP** marks the head bone for hitscan. Pair with skeleton or run alone if boxes feel too busy.

**Soft aimbot** is a smooth assist with FOV and strength sliders. It is not magic—tune it low in ranked if you want subtle help.
