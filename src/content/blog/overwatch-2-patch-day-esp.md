Blizzard patches **Overwatch 2** often. Every patch day, the same question hits Discord: “Is ESP up?”

## The honest timeline

1. **Patch drops** — game files change.
2. **Build goes offline** — injecting the old loader can crash OW2 or flag your account.
3. **We test** — small team, usually hours not minutes.
4. **Zadeyo posts “live”** — new download or auto-update inside the loader.

There is no magic “works mid-patch” mode. Anyone claiming zero downtime is lying.

## What you should do on patch day

| Do | Don't |
|----|--------|
| Check Zadeyo status page or Discord | Queue ranked with yesterday's build |
| Download the new loader when marked live | Share “fixed” DLLs from strangers |
| Read patch notes for OW2 balance only | Assume cheats update before the game stabilizes |

## How we communicate

- **Green / live** — safe to play with ESP per Zadeyo notice  
- **Yellow / testing** — internal QA, do not use on main account  
- **Red / offline** — wait

OverwatchCheats.com mirrors major status posts when we can—purchase and files still live on **[zadeyo.com](https://zadeyo.com/products/overwatch-2-novaxware)**.

## Will you lose subscription days?

That depends on Zadeyo policy at checkout. If downtime exceeds 24 hours, open a ticket on Zadeyo support with your receipt—do not chargeback first.

## After the new build is live

1. Close Overwatch and the old loader completely.
2. Download the update from Zadeyo (not a mirror site).
3. Re-apply your saved profiles—or import if the update preserved them.
4. Test in **practice range** before ranked.
