Bad settings waste a good **valorant esp** build. The video above shows a ranked-friendly layout: readable boxes, HP on the side, no screen-filling lines.

## Start here after install

After checkout on **[Zadeyo](https://zadeyo.com/valorant-xray)** and our [install guide](/blog/install-valorant-esp-windows), copy these defaults before you queue ranked.

### Ranked profile (`ranked`)

| Setting | Value | Why |
|---------|-------|-----|
| Enemy boxes | On, 1–2 px | See angles without blocking aim |
| Health bars | On, compact | Fast kill calls in voice |
| Distance | On | Falloff and rotate decisions |
| Snaplines | Off | Less noise in team fights |
| Team ESP | Off | Allies are already on your screen |
| Toggle key | `F7` or `F8` | Easy reach, hard to mis-press |

### Unrated profile (`unrated`)

- Slightly thicker boxes (2–3 px)
- Brighter color (still not full white)
- Same HP + distance—do not disable them for "cleaner" look

## Color picks that work on all maps

Valorant maps swing between bright (Haven) and dark (Pearl).

- **Primary enemy color:** `#d946ef` (magenta) or `#22d3ee` (cyan)
- **Avoid:** pure `#ffffff` boxes—they blow out on Bind
- **HP bar:** green → yellow → red gradient if the loader supports it; otherwise solid green is fine

Test one deathmatch in the shooting range after every color change.

## Sensitivity to your role

**Duelist (Jett, Reyna, Raze):**  
Keep distance on. You live by spacing and entry timing.

**Controller:**  
Wallhack matters more than distance—track pushes through smokes.

**Sentinel / Initiator:**  
HP bars help peel for teammates; do not max box size or you lose sight lines.

## Aimbot valorant settings

For **aimbot valorant** use in ranked:

- FOV: narrow (do not snap across the screen)
- Smoothing: high
- Triggerbot: off until you are comfortable with ESP alone

## When to change settings mid-season

Only tweak if:

- You changed monitor (1440p needs thinner boxes than 1080p)
- You switched agent pools drastically (entry → sentinel means different clutter tolerance)

Otherwise, stop touching sliders. Consistency beats "perfect" colors.

## Profiles on Zadeyo

1. Tune in the shooting range.
2. Click **Save profile** with a clear name.
3. Never edit during a competitive match—wait for spawn or use test modes.

If Zadeyo pushes a loader update, export or screenshot your settings. Rare updates reset local configs.
