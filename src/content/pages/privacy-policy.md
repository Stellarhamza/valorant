## Overview

**OverwatchCheats.com** ("we", "this site") publishes guides about Overwatch 2 ESP for Windows PC. We do not sell software on this domain—checkout and account data are handled on **[Zadeyo.com](https://zadeyo.com)** under their own policies.

## Information we collect

When you use OverwatchCheats.com we may collect:

- **Technical data** — IP address, browser type, pages viewed, and referral URL (standard server and analytics logs).
- **Contact email** — if you email support@overwatchcheats.com, we keep your message to reply.
- **Cookies** — for basic site function and analytics if enabled.

We do not ask for your Battle.net password or Overwatch account login on this site.

## How we use information

We use data to:

- Keep the site running and secure
- Answer support questions about our guides
- Understand which articles help players most

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing.

## Zadeyo.com purchases

If you buy ESP access, you leave OverwatchCheats.com and complete payment on **Zadeyo.com**. Read their privacy policy for billing, account, and loader-related data.

## Data retention

Support emails are kept only as long as needed to resolve your request. Server logs are rotated on a reasonable schedule.

## Your choices

You can:

- Stop using the site at any time
- Email **support@overwatchcheats.com** to ask about data we hold from your messages
- Use browser settings to block cookies (some features may not work)
